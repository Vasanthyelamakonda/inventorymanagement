Absolut vodka

Absolut Vodka is a Swedish vodka made exclusively from natural ingredients, with no added sugar and made from water and winter wheat grown in the fields of Åhus, Sweden. Absolut is rich becoming synonymous with art, culture and nightlife.

Ballentines

Ballantine's is a brand of blended Scotch whiskey produced in Scotland. The Ballantine's flavour is dependent on fingerprint malts from Miltonduff and Glenburgie, blended with 50 single malts and four single grains, selling over 110+ years in the market.

Barcardi

BACARDÍ Superior Rum is a light and aromatically balanced rum. Subtle notes of almonds and lime are complemented by hints of vanilla. The finish is dry, crisp, and clean.one of the largest privately held, family-owned spirits company.

Clement

Clement Shrubb is a liqueur based on unaged and six-year-old Clément Martinique agricole rum which is blended and then aged with orange peels in 250 litre oak casks for a year. The resulting blend is sweetened with cane syrup prior to bottling.

Dark matter

This orange & chocolate flavoured liqueur is a versatile choice for contemporary takes on classic cocktails such as Martinis and Sidecars, as well as mixing perfectly with ginger beer and lime for a simple, thirst-quenching serve.

8-PM

When the hand of clock strikes 8, its time to end the wait. Blended with rich quality grains, it unleashes carefree streak & engulfs one into an aura of unmatched experience which captures the true essence of the brand,world’s 11th largest selling whisky.
